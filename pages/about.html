About Us

Built by people who
understand the field.

AgroClimatic Risk Mapper started as a simple question: why doesn't every farmer have access to the same climate intelligence large agribusinesses do?

The Problem

Farming decisions still run
on guesswork.

Climate unpredictability is threatening agricultural productivity worldwide. Farmers face erratic weather with no early warning — and by the time a risk is visible, the crop is already affected.

  • No accessible AI tool exists for smallholder farmers to assess agro-climatic risk.
  • Traditional farming lacks real-time alerts and data-backed crop guidance.
  • Every degree of uncertainty translates directly into lost yield and income.

Our Mission

Put satellite-grade intelligence in every farmer's pocket.

We combine satellite imagery, IoT field sensors, and machine learning into one plain-language score — so a decision that used to need an agronomist can be made from a phone, in a language the farmer already speaks.

Hyperlocal field-level, not regional
Multilingual built for rural reach
